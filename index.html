About Taniti

Taniti is a small tropical island located in the Pacific Ocean with fewer than 500 square miles of natural beauty. Visitors can experience white sandy beaches, rocky coastlines, tropical rainforests, and an active volcano all on one island.

Home to approximately 20,000 residents, Taniti offers a welcoming atmosphere where visitors can experience authentic island culture while enjoying modern tourism amenities.

Island Highlights

🏖 Beaches

Relax on beautiful white sand beaches surrounding Yellow Leaf Bay.

🌋 Volcano

Visit Taniti's scenic active volcano located in the mountainous interior.

🌴 Rainforest

Explore hiking trails, zip-lining, and tropical wildlife.

🎣 Activities

Fishing tours, snorkeling, museums, helicopter rides, bowling, golf, pubs, art galleries, and much more.
