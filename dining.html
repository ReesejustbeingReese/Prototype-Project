Dining in Taniti

Experience a variety of delicious dining options, from authentic island cuisine to familiar international favorites.

Something for Every Appetite

Taniti offers a diverse selection of restaurants that showcase both local flavors and international cuisine. Whether you're looking for fresh seafood, American classics, or Asian-inspired dishes, you'll find plenty of great places to eat throughout the island.

🐟 Local Cuisine

Five restaurants specialize in traditional Tanitian meals featuring fresh fish, locally grown rice, tropical fruits, and island recipes.

🍔 American Favorites

Three restaurants serve familiar American-style meals including burgers, sandwiches, steaks, salads, and family-friendly menus.

🍜 Pan-Asian Cuisine

Two restaurants feature a variety of Asian-inspired dishes, including noodles, stir-fries, rice bowls, and fresh seafood.

Grocery & Convenience Stores

🛒 Supermarkets

Two full-service supermarkets provide groceries, fresh produce, beverages, and household essentials.

🏪 Grocery Stores

Two neighborhood grocery stores offer convenient shopping for visitors staying in hotels or vacation rentals.

🌙 Convenience Store

A 24-hour convenience store is available for snacks, drinks, travel necessities, and late-night purchases.

Dining Tips

  • ✔ Fresh seafood is one of Taniti's most popular specialties.
  • ✔ Many restaurants are located near Yellow Leaf Bay and Merriton Landing.
  • ✔ Reservations are recommended during peak tourist seasons.
  • ✔ Credit cards are accepted at most restaurants.
  • ✔ U.S. Dollars are the primary currency used throughout the island.

Don't Miss Local Favorites

Visitors are encouraged to try freshly caught fish, tropical fruit, coconut desserts, and traditional rice dishes for an authentic Tanitian dining experience.
