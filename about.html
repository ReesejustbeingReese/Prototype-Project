About Taniti

Discover the history, culture, and natural beauty of one of the Pacific's most unique tropical destinations.

Welcome to Paradise

Taniti is a beautiful tropical island located in the Pacific Ocean. Although the island covers less than 500 square miles, it offers an incredible variety of landscapes, including white sandy beaches, rocky coastlines, lush rainforests, and a mountainous interior with an active volcano.

Approximately 20,000 people call Taniti home. For generations, fishing and agriculture have been the island's primary industries. Today, tourism continues to grow while preserving the island's rich culture and welcoming community.
