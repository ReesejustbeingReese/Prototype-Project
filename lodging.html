Places to Stay

Whether you're looking for luxury accommodations or a budget-friendly place to rest, Taniti has lodging options for every traveler.

Find Your Perfect Stay

Taniti offers a variety of accommodations throughout the island. All lodging establishments are regularly inspected and regulated by the Tanitian government to ensure a safe and enjoyable stay.

🏨 Four-Star Resort

Enjoy luxury accommodations featuring ocean views, swimming pools, fine dining, spa services, and easy beach access.

🏡 Family-Owned Hotels

Experience warm island hospitality while staying in one of Taniti's charming locally owned hotels.

🍳 Bed & Breakfast

Relax in a cozy bed and breakfast offering homemade breakfasts and personalized service.

🎒 Budget Hostel

Perfect for backpackers and budget-conscious travelers, offering clean accommodations at affordable prices.

What You'll Enjoy

🛏 Comfortable Rooms

Clean, comfortable accommodations with modern amenities.

📶 Free Wi-Fi

Most hotels and resorts provide complimentary wireless internet.

🌊 Scenic Locations

Stay near beaches, shopping, restaurants, and Taniti's top attractions.

Travel Tips

  • ✔ Book early during the busy tourist season.
  • ✔ Many hotels are within walking distance of beaches and restaurants.
  • ✔ Family-owned hotels provide authentic island hospitality.
  • ✔ Budget accommodations are available for students and backpackers.
  • ✔ All lodging is inspected regularly for safety and quality.
